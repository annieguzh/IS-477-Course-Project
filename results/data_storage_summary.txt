DATA STORAGE AND ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW
=====================================

Filesystem layout:
  data/raw/        - Raw source files as downloaded (API dumps, original CSVs)
  data/processed/  - Cleaned and integrated datasets used for analysis
  results/         - Derived artifacts (summaries, plots, analysis outputs)

Naming conventions:
  * Raw files:        data/raw/<dataset_name>.csv
  * Processed files:  data/processed/<dataset_name>_cleaned.csv or integrated_*.csv
  * Checksums:        matching .sha256 files next to raw/processed data
  * Results:          results/*.csv, results/*.txt, results/*.png
